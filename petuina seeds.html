Seeds Sowing Now Simple and Easy With Creative Farmer Mix Agro peat or Manure with soil (30:70), this will help in germination of seeds. Now open the seed packet on a white sheet of paper as while opening the seeds might fall, which are not visible to naked eyes. After preparing the soil, insert the seeds into the prepared to half (0.5) inch of depth.
